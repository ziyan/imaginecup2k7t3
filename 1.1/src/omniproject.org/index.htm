Welcome to Omni!

Overview

Omni is a language learning system that uses social networking concepts to encourage student collaboration. Omni allows users to meet and communicate with each other in the language they are learning. Integrated into Omni are a set of features designed to help language learners of varying skill levels share information. When students encounter difficulties they can work past them by cooperating with other users. By allowing open access to Omni, other social networks and communication services will be able to use the power of the network to allow their users to communicate across language barriers. One of the greatest obstacles for language students is moving from the classroom to native speaking; Omni seeks to overcome these difficulties using social networking.

Things to do on Omni:

  • Meet someone new
  • Help someone with a translation
  • View the top translators on the network
  • Find someone you know
  • Send a message to a friend
  • Get help with a language problem
  • Tell Omni more about yourself
  • View translations you've been involved with in the past
  • Search translations that have already been completed
  • Get a quick automated translation

How do I use Omni?

  • It's a social network! Find friends, meet new people, and strike up a conversation with them. Don't worry about what language they speak, Omni is here to help you learn.
  • The big orange bar is called the Language Toolbar. Anytime you need help saying or understanding something in another language, use the tools it provides you.
  • Request and Perform translations often! When you can help someone learn, take the time to do it. When you have a question, don't be afraid to ask!
